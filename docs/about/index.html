About NOTF

What is Neighbourhoods of the Future?

NOTF is a network connecting communities, organizations, and individuals working on urban sustainability, climate resilience, and neighbourhood-level transformation across India.

We facilitate connections between those who have resources, expertise, and capacity (offers) with those who need them (asks), creating a collaborative ecosystem for community-led change.

Our Focus Areas

  • Urban Farming & Greening - Rooftop gardens, community farms, urban forests
  • Climate Resilience - Heat mitigation, water management, sustainable practices
  • Community Mobilization - Resident engagement, capacity building, local governance
  • Knowledge Sharing - Toolkits, playbooks, case studies, peer learning

How It Works

  1. Join the Network - Organizations and individuals can become members
  2. Post Asks & Offers - Share what you need and what you can provide
  3. Get Matched - Our system helps find relevant connections
  4. Collaborate - Work together on neighbourhood-level projects

The Nudge Unit

NOTF is coordinated by the Nudge Unit, which facilitates connections, maintains the network infrastructure, and supports collaborative initiatives.
