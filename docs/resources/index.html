Urban Farming Toolkit and Training

Offered by: Center for Civic Participation (C4C)

Location: Bengaluru (available for other communities)

What's Included

  • Plant selection guide for local climate
  • Soil preparation instructions
  • Irrigation system setup guide
  • Maintenance schedules
  • Troubleshooting guide
  • 2 hands-on training sessions (3 hours each)

Best For

  • Communities wanting to start rooftop/terrace gardens
  • Neighbourhoods addressing urban heat issues
  • Groups with identified spaces and interested residents

Requirements

  • Identified space (rooftop, terrace, or balconies)
  • Minimum 10 interested community members
  • Basic commitment to maintenance

Status: Available - Can support 2-3 communities at a time

Contact: contact@c4c.example.org

Funding for Pilot Rooftop Gardens in Vasanthnagar

Posted by: Center for Civic Participation (C4C)

Budget needed: Rs 50,000 - Rs 2,00,000 per site

Timeline: Q1-Q2 2025

Description

Seeking funding to establish 5 new rooftop gardens in Vasanthnagar as part of the urban heat mitigation program.

Per Garden Cost Breakdown

  • Materials (planters, soil, plants): Rs 30,000
  • Installation: Rs 15,000
  • Initial maintenance supplies: Rs 5,000

Expected Outcomes

  • 5 new rooftop gardens operational by June 2025
  • Temperature monitoring data collected
  • Community workshops conducted
  • Documentation for replication

Status: Open
