Join the NOTF Network

Connect your community or organization with the Neighbourhoods of the Future network.

NOTF brings together community organizations and neighborhoods working on place-based transformation. By joining, you'll:

  • 🤝 Connect with other communities and organizations
  • 📍 Get listed on our interactive map
  • 🎯 Share your asks and offers for collaboration
  • 💡 Access resources, playbooks, and toolkits
  • 🔍 Be discoverable through our search and matcher
