What is Neighbourhoods of the Future?

NOTF is a framework to encourage citizens at the local level to think about the common good, participate in steering infrastructure and behaviour, and build neighbourhoods that are resilient to climate shock and provide a better quality of life.

Community-Led Communities share knowledge, learn from each other, and imagine what their neighbourhood could be
Supported by Experts Solution providers offer ready-to-implement projects, training, and expertise to help communities act
Climate Resilient Track climate action across wards and sectors, from waste management to green cover to clean energy
