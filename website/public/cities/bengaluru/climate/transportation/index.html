Ward-Level Tracking

Transportation targets are tracked at the ward level across all 369 wards to ensure equitable access to sustainable mobility.

369 Wards Monitored

Each ward reports on public transport connectivity, cycling infrastructure, EV charging stations, and vehicle registration data. Ward-level data will be updated quarterly to track progress toward 2030 and 2035 targets.

Data Collection Status: Transit connectivity assessments underway. First ward-level reports expected Q2 2026.

Key Actions Being Taken

Initiatives and programs driving progress toward transportation sector targets.

Metro Expansion

Expanding Namma Metro network to 150km+ with ward-level last-mile connectivity planning.

EV Charging Network

Installing public EV charging stations in every ward with priority for commercial areas and transit hubs.

Cycling Infrastructure

Building protected cycling lanes on major corridors and bike-sharing systems in high-density wards.

Electric Bus Fleet

Transitioning BMTC buses to electric with dedicated depots and charging infrastructure.

Pedestrian-Friendly Streets

Creating wide sidewalks, safe crossings, and walkable neighborhoods in all wards.
