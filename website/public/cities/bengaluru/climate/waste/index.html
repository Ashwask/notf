Key Actions Being Taken

Initiatives and programs driving progress toward waste management sector targets.

Waste Segregation at Source

Color-coded bins (wet, dry, hazardous) distributed to all households with community training programs.

Community Composting

Setting up ward-level composting units and promoting home composting with subsidized equipment.

Dry Waste Collection Centers

Establishing collection points in every ward for recyclables with payment incentives for residents.

Single-Use Plastic Ban

Enforcing ban on single-use plastics with alternatives support for vendors and businesses.

Ward Waste Audits

Regular waste characterization studies to track segregation quality and identify improvement areas.
