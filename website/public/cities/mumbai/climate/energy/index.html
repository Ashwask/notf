Key Actions Being Taken

Initiatives and programs driving progress toward energy and building sector targets.

Clean Cooking Transition

Subsidized LPG connections and induction cookstoves for low-income households across all wards.

Rooftop Solar Program

Net metering policies and financing options for residential and commercial solar installations.

Energy-Efficient Appliances

Awareness campaigns and rebate programs for 5-star rated appliances and LED lighting.

Green Building Standards

Mandatory GRIHA/IGBC certification for new commercial buildings and incentives for retrofits.

Energy Audits

Free energy audits for residential complexes and commercial buildings to identify efficiency improvements.
