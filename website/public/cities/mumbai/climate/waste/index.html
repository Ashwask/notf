Key Actions Being Taken

Initiatives and programs driving progress toward waste management sector targets.

Source Segregation Programs

Community-level training and infrastructure for wet, dry, and hazardous waste separation at source.

Recycling Infrastructure

Material recovery facilities and recycling centers in every zone for efficient waste processing.

Composting Initiatives

Ward-level composting units and community composting programs to convert organic waste to nutrient-rich compost.

Waste-to-Energy Systems

Converting non-recyclable waste into electricity and heat through modern waste-to-energy plants.

Zero Waste Neighborhoods

Pilot programs creating model zero-waste neighborhoods through community engagement and circular economy practices.
