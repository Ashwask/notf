## How to Add Coordinates Manually

1. Go to: `http://localhost:8000/admin/communities.html`
2. Find the community in the list
3. Click "Edit"
4. Scroll to the bottom of the form
5. Enter Latitude and Longitude from this guide
6. Click "Save"

## Tips

- You can verify coordinates using Google Maps:
  - Search for the neighborhood
  - Right-click the location
  - Click on the coordinates to copy them

- Coordinates format: `latitude, longitude`
  - Latitude for Bangalore: ~12.9° to 13.1°
  - Longitude for Bangalore: ~77.4° to 77.8°
