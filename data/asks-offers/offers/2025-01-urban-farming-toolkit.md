## Description

We offer a comprehensive urban farming toolkit and training program that includes:
- Plant selection guide for local climate
- Soil preparation instructions
- Irrigation system setup
- Maintenance schedules
- Troubleshooting guide

Plus 2 hands-on training sessions (3 hours each) for community members.

## Best For

- Communities wanting to start rooftop/terrace gardens
- Neighborhoods addressing urban heat issues
- Groups with identified spaces and interested residents
- Projects with basic budget for materials

## Requirements

- Identified space (rooftop, terrace, or balconies)
- Minimum 10 interested community members
- Basic commitment to maintenance
- Materials budget (we can provide cost estimates)
