## Description

We're seeking funding to establish 5 new rooftop gardens in Vasanthnagar as part
of our urban heat mitigation program. Each garden requires:
- Materials (planters, soil, plants): ₹30,000
- Installation: ₹15,000
- Initial maintenance supplies: ₹5,000

We've already identified 8 interested building owners and have trained volunteers
ready to maintain the gardens.

## Ideal Match

- Grant amount: ₹50,000 - ₹2,00,000 (for 1-4 sites)
- Flexible reporting requirements
- Open to co-funding arrangements
- Understanding of climate/community work

## Expected Outcome

- 5 new rooftop gardens operational by June 2025
- Temperature monitoring data collected
- Community workshops conducted
- Documentation for replication in other neighborhoods
