## About JP nagar

A group of women from J P Nagar Bangalore working for welfare of the residents and administration of civic, social and cultural activities

## Lead Organization

**Women of Wisdom** is leading community initiatives in JP nagar.

## What the Community Offers

- Leads work on waste management, community-led segregation, and mobility.

Strong on-ground presence and community mobilisation → helps drive behaviour change and implementation.

Runs awareness sessions on sustainable menstruation in schools.
