description: "Founded in May 2019, although C4C has members from across Bengaluru,\
  \ since many are from Vasanthanagar, we have focussed in some matters on Vasanthanagar\
  \ including as a pilot for several projects. The aim is to make citizens understand\
  \ what they can achieve with awareness, initiatives and collaboration in order to\
  \ improve quality of life in their own neighbourhoods. \nOne of the first projects\
  \ taken up by C4C was to do with Streetlights, to move towards our goal of zero\
  \ dark spot, since any dark spot is a potential place for accidents, crime and anti-social\
  \ activities. We first collected relevant data after dividing Vasanthanagar into\
  \ suitable zones. This was collated and discussed with concerned BBMP Engineer and\
  \ Contractor, along with our requirements in terms of new poles, new streetlight\
  \ fittings, new timers, pruning of tree-branches, etc. in addition to repairing\
  \ defective lights and timers. We formed a small team which coordinated with the\
  \ Officials and Contractors along with regular monitoring and feedback.  There were\
  \ challenges – like Bengaluru’s new Smart LED project which actually disrupted all\
  \ the work done in Vasanthanagar, which was chosen as a pilot by the BBMP. But we\
  \ did not give up. Luckily, that programme was cancelled by the Government and we\
  \ started all over again. With over 30 new poles and over 50 new Streetlights and\
  \ regular pruning of tree-branches as well as monitoring by Team C4C, Vasanthanagar\
  \ has indeed moved closer to a zero dark spot area. This model, having been covered\
  \ by media, drew attention of concerned citizens from other localities for emulation.\
  \ \nSimilarly, Team C4C has been working on traffic, afforestation, plastic waste,\
  \ vehicular noise pollution, roads, footpaths, elimination of black and yellow spots,\
  \ and so on, with focus on local area improvements in collaboration with relevant\
  \ officials and also with the MLA, because there are no Councillors in Bengaluru\
  \ since 2020. Several of our conceptual designs of solutions for smoother traffic\
  \ flow have been implemented. Team C4C takes care of a dense urban forest in Vasanthanagar\
  \ planted with about 3000 trees in 2019. We organise awareness and cleanliness drives\
  \ with students of nearby educational institutions. We also organise awareness campaigns\
  \ regarding honking with students after making them understand the various aspects.\
  \ We work closely with officials of various departments in the local area to ensure\
  \ better roads, footpaths and garbage removal. \nApart from a sense of achievement,\
  \ the exercise also empowers citizens and makes it easier for appropriate services\
  \ to be actually rendered more efficiently by concerned officials as needed by residents,\
  \ while helping create an eco-system conducive for larger citizen participation\
  \ in a democratic setup. This is in tune with the tagline of C4C to ‘leave our surroundings\
  \ a little better than we found it”."
