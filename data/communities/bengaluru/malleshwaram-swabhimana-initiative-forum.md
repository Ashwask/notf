  They meet on the first Wednesday of every month at 4:30, at Hymamshu Vidyapeeta
  with the civic department officials to share and get our problems solved.

  MSIF acts as a bridge between the citizens and department officials to get civic
  problems solved.

  Ongoing activities almost on a day to day basis towards keeping Malleshwaram Cleaner,
  Greener and Safer - interact with BBMP on issues of no garbage pickup, black spots,
  potholes, digging without permissions, road and footpath encroachments.


  Malleshwaram Swabhimanaa Initiative (MSI) is a Community based Organization of Malleshwaram.
  MSI works towards solving civic issues with a definite geographic boundary of Malleswaram
  and for the welfare of the residents of Malleshwaram. MSI have been associated on
  several transport related initiatives such as enabling Malleswaram to have pothole
  free roads and walkable footpaths. The have organized 11 Cycle Day since 2014 in
  association with DULT and BCOS. MSI has been engaged in organizing Walkathons on
  Sundays to identify problems in the area as well as to encourage walking. MSI has
  also been instrumental in preparation of Malleshwaram Accessibility Project (MAP).
  Moreover, they have proposed a Hop on Hop off Bus for Malleswaram to connect the
  residents to the nearby (two) metro stations.'
