## About the Community

Vasanthnagar is a centrally located neighborhood in Bengaluru with a mix of 
residential buildings and commercial establishments. The community has been 
experiencing increasing urban heat due to reduced green cover.

## Current Initiatives

**Rooftop Gardens Program**
Led by C4C, the community has established 15+ rooftop gardens focusing on:
- Native plant species
- Water conservation
- Urban cooling
- Community gathering spaces

## Key Challenges

- Limited space for greening
- Building permission processes
- Water availability
- Maintenance of existing gardens

## Achievements

- 15 rooftop gardens installed
- 50+ residents trained
- Measurable temperature reduction (3-5°C)
- Strong community participation
